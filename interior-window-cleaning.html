Our Process

1

Protection & Prep

We protect furniture, floors, and sills before we begin. Our crew is careful around your belongings.

2

Clean & Scrub

We apply a professional interior glass cleaner and scrub away dust, fingerprints, and grime.

3

Squeegee & Polish

Every pane is squeegeed to a streak-free finish, then polished with a microfiber cloth.

4

Sills, Tracks & Final Check

We wipe down sills and tracks and do a final walk-through so every window shines.
